North American P-51 Mustang

The premier fighter of World War 2

P-51 Mustang: The most successful fighter aircraft of WWII

  • Manufactured by North American Aviation in Inglewood, California
  • First airframe designed and built in 102 days
  • First flight October 26, 1940
  • Officially served from 1942 to 1984
  • Used by air forces all over the world
  • Originally designed as a long range bomber escort
  • Revolutionary laminar flow wing provided low drag at high speed
  • Over 15,000 produced
  • Max speed 440 mph
  • Range 1,650 mi
  • Armed with six .50 caliber machine guns
  • Wingspan 37 ft 0 in
  • Length 32 ft 3 in
